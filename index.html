New Game button: Pressing New Game logs the current game scores to the History page and starts a new game. You will see no changes on the Scoreboard after pushing it.

Adding ScoreBG to your Home Screen: The app is available at vanalstk.github.io/ScoreBG. To create a button on your home screen, load the URL in Safari, tap the Share button (the box with an arrow pointing upward), scroll down until you see Add to Home Screen and tap it, then tap Add in the top right corner.

Compatibility: ScoreBG was optimized for Safari on an iPhone. It may not work well on other platforms.
